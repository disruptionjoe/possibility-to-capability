# Referee report: CompletionClass-P2C v0.1, derived firewall, composite attack on P2C-W1

Charge: attack the firewall "derivation" (is it a relabeled stipulation? is
it circular?), the composition-closure result (is it evidence or
construction?), and the controls (do the kill conditions have teeth in BOTH
directions?). The Neutrality Rule binds: do not root for the scoped survivor
surviving the composite attack, and do not root for the firewall being
derivable. Corrections marked APPLIED were made before the final commit;
this report GOVERNS where they conflict.

## Findings

### R1 (HIGH) — Is the derivation circular: do the axioms smuggle the firewall in? NOT SUSTAINED as a kill; SUSTAINED as a permanent scoping demand. APPLIED.

The sharpest attack: A-CAL ("the verdict map must not be constant") could be
the firewall wearing a costume — pick axioms until the absorber loses.

Adjudication: three facts block the kill. (i) The axioms are TYPE-FREE: none
mentions whole-family, containment, or any constructor; the cap falls out of
constructor FACTS (hull totality f1, statelessness t1, in-frame
unrealizability c1/c2) meeting the axioms. (ii) All three axioms have
pre-existing provenance that PREDATES this swing and predates the TI import:
A-FRAME is the frozen witness's frame declaration plus the adapter's
`RESOURCE_FRAME_CHANGED` typing (commit 850521c); A-UNIF is the adapter's
verdict-carrying-input rejection (850521c); A-CAL is verbatim the item's own
pre-declared kill condition. They were not fitted to the desired conclusion.
(iii) The refutation branch was actually run, not waved at: the unrestricted
absorber (hull granted operational strength) reopens absorption AND is
exhibited to collapse the instrument (`f2-fail`).

HOWEVER, the demand stands permanently: what is derived is "firewall, GIVEN
that the class functions as a non-constant, outcome-independent,
frame-preserving diagnostic instrument." Instrument admissibility is a
methodological commitment, not a theorem. A rival standard may bite that
bullet; it is exhibited-collapsed, not refuted. The verdict must NEVER be
quoted as "firewall derived" without this scope, and C2 must be reported as
NARROWED (from v1-table-relativity to axiom-relativity), never as
eliminated. SYNTHESIS sections 2 and 6 carry this. APPLIED (verified in
final text).

### R2 (HIGH) — Is the composite no-go evidence or construction? SUSTAINED in part. APPLIED.

The model has exactly TWO value-bearing carriers and only THREE reachable
composite profiles. The carrier dichotomy (c2) is close to a consequence of
the state-space design; an 819-composite "exhaustive search" over a
3-profile space is combinatorially trivial. If SYNTHESIS claimed D2 closed
with combinatorial force, that would overclaim.

Adjudication: within the model, the checks are genuine (the matcher could
have surprised — `c1-fail` proves it has teeth by exhibiting the absorber
that appears the moment one signature is dropped; `c4` proves the closure is
a fixpoint rather than an enumeration hope). The physical force rests
entirely on the carrier-exhaustiveness commitment (no locally invariant,
flux-tracking, zero-maintenance channel in a normal conductor), which is
literature-grade — exactly the grade of the frozen `k1` the composite result
extends. Correction APPLIED: SYNTHESIS section 3 now states the epistemic
parity explicitly ("closes D2 at exactly the grade at which D2 was opened,
no higher"), and section 6 carries the 3-profile coarseness note. With that
scoping the result stands: D2's question was never about combinatorics; it
was whether composite absorbers were UNTESTED. They are now tested, at
parity grade, with the margin honestly recorded.

### R3 (MEDIUM) — Certified-family membership: is admitting the S-phase profile outcome-dependent smuggling? SUSTAINED as a residual to record. APPLIED.

`S_PHASE_PROFILE` equals the witness's target profile by definition, so

An earlier draft was worse (admit-by-NAME); that was corrected to structural
profile membership before freeze. The remaining question is whether the

1957, fluxoid quantization 1961) decades before this witness was scored, and
TI's v1 carries the same honest limit (family legitimacy is scientific
input, not classifier output). In-model it is a declaration. Correction
APPLIED: recorded in SYNTHESIS section 6. Residual: general
family-exhaustiveness remains unproved (unchanged from TI R-2).

### R4 (MEDIUM) — The f3-fail servo mutant is a constructed collapse. NOT SUSTAINED beyond the recorded note.

`servo_absorbs` grants the A-FRAME-violating mutant any invariant-persistent

SYNTHESIS section 6 records the grade. Acceptable at exploration tier.
