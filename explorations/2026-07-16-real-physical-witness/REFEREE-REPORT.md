## (2) Defects and dispositions

**R-D1 — MODERATE/SUBSTANTIVE — the matched before/after was two different
comparisons conflated.** The success condition asks for "matched before and
after descriptions." The literal single-system before/after (same ring, N vs S)
sits at two temperatures and is NOT budget-matched; the budget-matched
comparison (ring in S vs a reference normal conductor at the same `T_f`) is
matched but is a COUNTERFACTUAL PAIR, not one system's two times. Left
conflated, the freeze could be read as claiming a frame-free matched
before/after it does not have. *Correction (applied):* both documents now label
the two comparisons explicitly and scope the capability claim as **frame-indexed
to the matched-budget counterfactual pair** (Hierarchy v0.2 §2.5, frame as
parameter). Refutation attempt: the referee tried to show the resource attack
wins once the comparison is honestly counterfactual — it does not, because a
reference normal conductor at the same `T_f` and budget still cannot hold the
zero-cost persistent quantized memory; the capability gap is the phase, not the
matched cooling. The correction narrows the claim's scope without killing it.

**R-D2 — MINOR — the resource-completion steelman was under-built in the
fixture.** The fixture's `resource` completion drives a decaying continuous
current; a stronger resource steelman could pump an integer-valued flux.
*Correction (applied here):* noted that even an integer-valued pumped current in
N fails signatures (I) and (P) — it is not invariant under local ops and it
decays — so the resource leg fails on persistence/invariance regardless of
whether its VALUE happens to be integer. The fixture's continuous model is a
sufficient (not the only) witness; the prose no longer implies value-continuity
is the sole resource defeater.

**R-D3 — MINOR — neutrality: choosing (Q,I,P) as the capability marker.** A
skeptic could read the selection of quantization+invariance+persistence as the
verdict smuggled in. Cleared: (Q,I,P) are native observable signatures (fluxoid
staircase, homotopy invariance, decay time), not verdict tokens; the
disposition licenses NO enlargement (it is UNDISCHARGED); `n1`/`n1-fail`
machine-check label-swap invariance of the discriminator verdict with a
demonstrated failing direction. The verdict does not depend on rooting for
capability enlargement — indeed the swing's own conclusion withholds it.

**R-D4 — MINOR — persistence grade.** "Zero maintenance cost forever" is a
thermodynamic-limit idealization; at finite ring size phase slips are not
literally forbidden. Cleared as already conceded (WITNESS-FREEZE §4 caveat 2,
SYNTHESIS §5): signature (P) is graded "effectively infinite (literature)", not
"provably infinite", and the history leg is capped but not fully killed.

**R-D5 — MINOR — no `literature/` novelty-audit entry.** Physics is cited inline
at literature grade (named results and discoverers: BCS 1957, London 1948,
Deaver–Fairbank / Doll–Näbauer 1961, GL 1950), which is adequate for an
exploration-tier freeze, but a dedicated `literature/` prior-art map was not
written. *Disposition:* advisory only; recorded as Track-belt follow-up, not a
blocker for this swing (the swing's minimum material output — an executable
discriminator plus a source-grounded candidate + rival — is met).
