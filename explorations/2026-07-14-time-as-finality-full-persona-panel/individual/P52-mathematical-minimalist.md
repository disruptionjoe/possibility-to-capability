# P52 — Mathematical Minimalist

## Steelman thesis

The smallest defensible core is not a six-step hierarchy but a partial classifier on frozen cases, together with an equivalence relation on descriptions, a comparison relation on operational task sets, and an obstruction value when factorization or continuation fails. Everything else should earn its place by distinguishing cases that this smaller object cannot.

## Summary of understanding

The repository already retreats from a ladder: types can be skipped, co-located, incomparable, construction-indexed, or revised. Source packets are immutable; receiver gates cannot upgrade them. The synthetic suite shows that stipulated witnesses can keep record separate from access, access separate from normalized capability, and irreversibility separate from candidate finality. No real physical case has passed. GU-001 is provenance-blocked, so it contributes no receiver-side evidence beyond demonstrating the stop rule.

The mathematical nucleus appears to be: a set or category of cases; admissible reformulations; a family of constructions; predicates for persistent distinction and operational access; normalized task sets with equality, inclusion, or incomparability; and a scoped extension test. “Possibility” and “dynamics” may parameterize the cases rather than constitute peer labels. “Finality” may be a meta-status of the extension search rather than a sixth physical type.

## Strongest insight

The best move is the explicit rejection of total order. Once nulls, forks, and incomparability are admitted, the instrument can be judged as a partial algebra rather than defended as a universal story. That makes redundancy and independence questions tractable.

## Strongest criticism

The six nouns are heterogeneous. Possibility names a domain, dynamics a relation, record a stable physical correlate, access an agent-system relation, capability a normalized task-set comparison, and finality a verdict about failed factorization and continuation. Placing them at one taxonomic level may create complexity without mathematical unity. The hierarchy could be a presentation of two or three primitives plus derived predicates.

## Hidden assumptions

It assumes a common case identity across descriptions, a justified equivalence relation, a stable task vocabulary, and a normalization operator that is idempotent and non-conclusion-entailing. It assumes factorization searches can be ordered by strength and that continuation classes form a coherent closure operation. None of these has been established on a real packet.

## Rose

The repository permits `HIERARCHY_REVISION`. That is essential: a minimal theory must be allowed to discover that its chosen generators are redundant, incomplete, or badly typed.

## Bud

The most promising development is an ablation study of the vocabulary. Compare the six-type classifier with smaller algebras and retain a type only if it yields an invariant discrimination, prediction, or compression that no combination of the others reproduces.

## Thorn

The dangerous failure is qualification accretion. Every counterexample may be answered with another subtype, gate, or construction annotation until the instrument becomes unfalsifiably expressive and no longer compresses anything.

## Confidence

8/10 that a smaller formal core is the best minimalist interpretation. The rating concerns conceptual economy. Synthetic separability does not prove logical independence, and zero real runs means empirical sufficiency is wholly open.

## Suggested experiment

On the first several accepted packets, preregister three models: the full six-type diagnostic, a three-part model of admissibility/effective operation/obstruction, and a dynamics-plus-context baseline. Compare blinded classification stability, intervention prediction, description length, and source-semantic fidelity. Count abstentions and revisions rather than excluding them.

## Suggested theorem or mathematical direction

Define the free partial algebra generated by reformulation, evolution, persistence, access, task comparison, and scoped closure. Prove which named diagnoses are independent and which are definable from others under explicit axioms. Seek a minimal sufficient quotient preserving every validated discrimination. If finality is retained, define it as a relative obstruction operator, not an unexplained primitive.

## Suggested falsification test

Reject the six-type presentation if a strictly smaller classifier reproduces every real-case verdict and prediction with equal or better reformulation invariance. Reject the normalization if it is non-idempotent, order-dependent, or changes under an admissible equivalence. Repeated such failures should simplify the hierarchy, not decorate it.

## Relationship classification

`reduction`. The minimalist hypothesis is that the candidate can be reduced to a smaller partial algebra with several labels derived rather than primitive. This is a proposed mathematical direction, not an established result; real packets could show the reduction loses essential structure.
