# Referee report — P2C-XFRAME-001

## Governing verdict

**Accept `INDEXED_CONSTRUCTION_FORK` at finite-model exploration grade.** The
run cleanly separates certified representation invariance from material
construction change. It does not earn invariant descent, a physics theorem, or
a universal capability ontology.

## Corrections binding on citation

1. **The primitive profile is a candidate common refinement, not a proved
   quotient.** Its existence follows from the receiver's schema. Its physical
   or cross-domain adequacy requires explicit faithful maps.
2. **N, R, and W are not source-certified equivalents.** Agreement of N and R
   on P2C-W1 is output coincidence under one stipulated interface, not proof
   that the constructions are identical. W is a material fork.
3. **The top-level label does not descend.** The access-only control produces
   `ACCESS_CHANGE` under N and conservative capability extension under R from
   the same primitive facts. Preserve this disagreement.
4. **F5 did not fire.** The only initial F5 token was caused by an invalid
   relabel loader that changed an omitted primitive fact. The incident is
   retained; the interpretation repair follows frozen P4 and changes no
   decision rule.
5. **Evidence counts are not independence counts.** Most passing checks are
   finite model executions over stipulated profiles. The byte-hash and packet
   checks establish input identity and sovereignty, not physical truth.
6. **C1 and the persistence cap remain binding.** The P2C-to-TaF task mapping
   is not independently established, and `tau_P` is not unbounded.
7. **Frame R remains live.** The run has not exercised the rich contextuality,
   self-application, or universal-property falsifiers required to validate or
   reject R beyond this construction.
8. **No source truth or finality moved.** The result is P2C-owned synthesis
   only. No source repository was edited and finality is `NOT_REACHED`.

## Strongest counter-reading

The same finite facts may be represented economically as reachability in one
augmented controlled dynamics containing system, interface, resource,
controller, record, and verifier states. If every typed diagnosis factors
through a task-and-verifier-preserving bisimulation quotient, “Capability” may
be explanatory bookkeeping rather than an independently forced level. This is
not yet shown; it is the strongest adversarial successor.

## Required next evidence for a stronger claim

- explicit start-preserving embeddings and restriction maps;
- independently fixed carrier-neutral task semantics;
- full interface identity rather than equal readout labels;
- vector resource and horizon/error Pareto frontiers;
- preparation/evaluation stage separation;
- exhaustive finite policy closure; and
- an augmented-dynamics quotient tested against the same raw transitions.

Until those exist, cite the result only as: **a bounded common refinement with
representation invariance and a retained construction-indexed label fork.**
