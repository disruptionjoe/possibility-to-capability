# Paper Candidates

Candidate papers are banked research byproducts, not accepted claims or
external publications.

Each candidate should record:

- the Track-1 question or branch that produced it;
- construction, assumptions, evidence grade, and verification status;
- what is familiar, newly assembled, synthetically supported, blocked, and
  genuinely open;
- source-repository ownership and required frozen packets;
- falsifiers, nonclaims, and reopening conditions; and
- the exact gate required before external publication or stronger language.
