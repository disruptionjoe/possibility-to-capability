# A Coherent Story From Possibility to Capability

## Candidate answer in plain English

The strongest story this repository can currently defend is not that reality
moves through six stages. It is that physical explanations answer several
different kinds of question which are easy to blur together.

Physics first specifies what is admissible: the states, histories, fields,
constraints, or alternatives the theory permits. Dynamics says how a system
can move or transform within an assessed family. Some transformations stabilize
distinctions into records. A record affects later events only through some
access, conditioning, observability, reachability, or control relation.
Changed access can make more actions executable without changing what the
system can do after descriptions, resources, access, and control are held fixed
under a declared comparison. That normalized operational possibility set is
what this construction calls capability. Finality should enter only as a
candidate diagnosis when a real settlement or selection cannot, under a named
search scope and retained constructions, be factored through possibility,
dynamics, records, access, or capability and is not reopened by an admissible
continuation.

These are diagnostic types, not a chronology. One event may instantiate
several, skip some, or force a revision of the vocabulary. The value of the
story is therefore discriminative: it asks exactly what changed, what merely
became visible, what became actionable, what genuinely enlarged or restricted
normalized operation, and what—if anything—remains after every preceding
explanation has had its strongest hearing.

That is a coherent candidate understanding of the larger picture. It is not
yet a universal physical result, a theory of everything, or evidence that
irreducible finality exists.

## Falsifiable guiding question

**Can a representation-respecting typed decomposition of physical cases into
admissible possibility, fixed-family dynamics, record formation, access,
normalized capability, and candidate finality only after explicit
factorization tests classify real frozen evidence more stably and
discriminatively than its strongest reductive, context-relative, and pluralist
rivals—or do real cases collapse the distinctions, change under admissible
construction, or expose a missing type?**

This question permits the candidate to lose. A real case may factor entirely
through ordinary dynamics, make access constitutive of capability, require
several incommensurable explanatory grammars, or return
`HIERARCHY_REVISION`.

## What kind of object this is

The arrow in possibility -> dynamics -> records -> access -> capability ->
finality is a **typing and diagnostic relation**, not an assertion of temporal,
causal, metaphysical, or logical succession.

- A case need not visit every type.
- Multiple types may be co-located in one event.
- Some types may be incomparable in a particular construction.
- An admissible construction fork may yield different diagnoses.
- `FINALITY_CANDIDATE` is weaker than finality.
- `HIERARCHY_REVISION` is a successful diagnosis of instrument failure, not a
  failed research run.

The preferred candidate is therefore a typed partial diagnostic. Treating it
as a universal ladder would contradict the repository's own synthetic result.

## Epistemic separation

### 1. Familiar conceptual ingredients

The following ingredients are familiar across established physical and
mathematical practice. This repository does not claim to have invented them:

- admissible state, history, field, constraint, and model spaces;
- dynamical laws, transition rules, kernels, and trajectories;
- persistent and discriminable physical records;
- observability, controllability, reachability, conditioning, and information
  access;
- operational and resource-relative task sets; and
- boundary conditions, settlement, irreversibility, and open questions about
  closure or the arrow of time.

Calling these familiar is an orientation statement, not a completed literature
claim. A publication-grade synthesis still needs a scoped literature map and
priority audit.

### 2. This repository's new assembly and diagnostic machinery

What is new here is the particular assembly and its error controls, not the raw
ingredients:

- treating the six terms as non-exclusive typed diagnoses rather than a
  chronology;
- distinguishing fixed-family disclosure from normalized capability change;
- separating record persistence from access and raw task availability from
  capability under a named normalization frame;
- admitting finality only as a candidate after settlement, scoped
  non-factorization, and admissible-continuation tests;
- freezing source packets with reproducible bundle identity and explicit
  method-premise dependencies;
- keeping receiver interpretation separate and unable to strengthen source
  wording or status;
- requiring one coherent argument and construction branch across the gate
  chain, rather than combining partial passes;
- preserving failure, blocking, uncertainty, construction forks, nulls,
  incomparability, and hierarchy revision; and
- testing label reversal without pretending that a label swap proves full
  representation invariance.

These are provisional repo-owned contracts and instruments. Novelty in
assembly does not imply that the resulting physical story is true.

### 3. Provisional synthetic results

At deterministic-synthetic grade, Swings 1-3 established only that:

- the packet contract can make identity and method dependence explicit in its
  controlled fixture;
- the eight-gate engine preserves `PASS`, `FAIL`, `BLOCKED`, and
  `INDETERMINATE`, blocks skipped prerequisites, rejects split-chain success,
  and detects one class of label sensitivity;
- the transition vocabulary remains mechanically distinguishable across 35
  synthetic cases and 16 aggregate outcomes;
- record need not imply access, access need not imply normalized capability,
  and irreversibility need not imply finality in those controls; and
- the hierarchy survives those controls only as a fallible typed diagnostic,
  not as a strict sequence.

Synthetic controls validate the instrument's behavior under stipulated
witnesses. They do not establish any physical witness, ontology, or universal
classification.

### 5. Genuinely open claims

No current repo evidence establishes that:

- the six types are complete, universal, fundamental, or ontologically real;
- the diagnosis is invariant under substantive admissible reformulations;
- access can always be normalized away from capability;
- operational task sets admit a source-independent common comparison frame;
- a real physical case passes the complete frozen-packet and gate sequence;
- an apparent finality cannot be reduced to enlarged dynamics,
  coarse-graining, boundary conditions, records, access, or capability;
- GU's founding seam supports any receiver-side physical conclusion;
- TI or TaF supplies the selecting datum for any accepted packet; or
- one plain-English story should replace domain-specific physical theories.

## Why the candidate is plausible

The candidate is plausible for three limited reasons.

First, it organizes familiar explanatory questions without requiring a new
substance or force. It asks whether a change is in the admissible family, the
lawful transformation, the persistence of a distinction, its operational
availability, the normalized task set, or an apparently non-factorable
settlement.

Second, the distinctions have already survived strong synthetic false-positive
controls. In the implemented construction, descriptions do not automatically
become capabilities, records do not automatically become access, executable
tasks do not automatically become normalized capability, and irreversibility
does not automatically become finality.

Third, the architecture is designed to lose honestly. Source packets can be
blocked, gate chains can fail, construction branches can disagree, and a real
case can revise the hierarchy. A story with explicit losing conditions is more
credible than one that redescribes every outcome as confirmation.

Plausibility is not evidential confirmation. No real accepted packet has yet
classified a physical transition.

## Why the research program is feasible

The test program is feasible because the repo now has explicit interfaces for
packet identity, source/receiver separation, method dependence, gate outcomes,
transition witnesses, construction forks, and negative results. Once a source
issues a valid frozen packet, the next receiver actions are concrete and
bounded rather than interpretive improvisation.

The main feasibility constraint is upstream evidence preparation. GU-001 is
blocked because the required source-issued object does not yet exist. A second
constraint is scientific rather than technical: normalization frames and
factorization searches may be theory-laden and require independent review.

## Strong rival stories

### Rival 1: Reductive dynamics and coarse-graining are enough

The strongest reductive account says that a sufficiently complete state space,
dynamics, boundary conditions, and coarse-graining explain every record,
access relation, task set, apparent selection, and arrow of time. Capability is
a convenient summary of reachable states under constraints. Finality adds no
new explanatory kind; it names either effective irreversibility or ignorance of
the larger dynamical model.

This rival is favored if every candidate finality factors through a defensible
enlarged dynamics or boundary condition; if record, access, and capability
diagnoses add no stable prediction or compression beyond that model; if
normalization choices are derivable from the dynamics rather than independently
defended; or if apparent closure reopens in an admissible embedding.

### Rival 2: Operational context is primary

The strongest operational-context account says there is no context-independent
capability set waiting to be discovered. Tasks, agents, resources, access,
controls, and success criteria jointly constitute capability. Trying to hold
access fixed may remove the very relation that makes a capability meaningful.
The hierarchy's access/capability boundary is therefore a useful bookkeeping
choice in some settings, not a general physical distinction.

This rival is favored if equally admissible observer, task, resource, or control
frames yield different capability diagnoses with no invariant quotient; if
access-constitutive cases repeatedly return construction forks; if the common
normalization predetermines the verdict; or if context-relative predictions
outperform the normalized hierarchy.

### Rival 3: Explanatory plurality is irreducible

The strongest pluralist account says physics may not admit one universal plain-
English grammar. Dynamics, thermodynamics, information, control, quantum
measurement, spacetime, and agency may support locally successful but
non-reducible explanatory structures. The six-type vocabulary can still be a
useful comparison tool, but repeated `HIERARCHY_REVISION` is a discovery that
there is no single joint object, not a temporary defect to repair away.

This rival is favored if well-specified real cases require stable missing types;
if cross-domain mappings erase load-bearing distinctions or predictions; if no
shared normalization or gate vocabulary survives; or if multiple incompatible
taxonomies remain equally invariant and empirically adequate.

### Rival 4: Constraint and consistency are more fundamental than the arc

The strongest structural rival says the apparent hierarchy is a projection of
one deeper question: which global structures are mathematically and physically
consistent? Possibility, dynamics, records, access, capability, and settlement
are different views of constraint satisfaction. The explanatory arrow should
therefore not run from possibility to finality at all.

This rival is favored if the six diagnoses can be derived as representation-
dependent projections of one invariant constraint object; if construction
forks collapse under that object; if the constraint formulation predicts the
same discriminations more simply; or if the typed vocabulary adds no cases the
constraint account cannot classify.

## Confidence calibration

- **Diagnostic coherence:** moderate confidence. The distinctions are explicit
  and survived a substantial synthetic suite, but no real accepted packet has
  tested them.
- **Usefulness as a cross-repository interface:** moderate confidence. The
  blocked preflight demonstrates disciplined stopping; interoperability after
  acceptance remains untested.
- **Universality or completeness:** low confidence and currently unsupported.
- **Physical irreducibility of finality:** not assessable from current repo
  evidence. No finality gate has run on a real packet.
- **GU as a worked confirmation:** no confidence assignment is admissible here
  because GU-001 is not imported and all substantive gates are unrun.

No numerical likelihood is assigned. The repo evidence is contract and
synthetic-test evidence, not a sampling frame from which a defensible
probability can be estimated.

## What would kill the candidate

The candidate should be rejected or substantially rewritten if one or more of
these patterns survives serious review:

1. diagnoses change under admissible reformulation rather than only under a
   genuinely different construction;
2. access and normalized capability cannot be separated without circularly
   assuming the desired answer;
3. every finality candidate factors through a defensible earlier-layer model;
4. a simpler reductive or constraint account matches every discrimination and
   predicts more;
5. multiple real cases produce the same stable missing explanatory type;
6. one-chain success cannot represent scientifically valid multi-argument
   support without either rejecting it or aggregating artificially;
7. source packets cannot cross repo boundaries without changing their meaning
   or authority; or
8. no real packet can survive the full intake and gate sequence.

## Nonclaims

- This is not a unified theory, a new fundamental law, or a chronology of the
  universe.
- The six types are not claimed to be mutually exclusive or exhaustive.
- Capability is defined operationally for this construction, not asserted as a
  new physical substance.
- Candidate finality is not proof of finality, free will, collapse, temporal
  issuance, or cosmological closure.
- Familiar ingredients are not claimed as novel.
- Synthetic contract validation is not empirical evidence.
- GU, TI, TaF, and WI-069 claims and status remain entirely with their owners.
