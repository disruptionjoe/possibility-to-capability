## 0. The theorem, plain-English form

> A physics program (GU) reduced its remaining ambiguity to one binary datum: the sign that decides which
> half of an indefinite-metric structure is "physical." Independent formal programs (TI, TaF) admit
> encodings onto one shared signed-graph input on which all three compute the same *consistency* predicate
> (agreement partly guaranteed by the shared encoding) — and at every tier modeled, **the consistency
> structure carries the sign only as a label: nothing label-free in it can determine the sign's value, and
> the one place the label is read (shipped T26's string-order atom) is a label-reading atom, not a
> derivation.** Deriving the value provably requires — at every tier modeled, and provably within the
> stipulated finality/issuance-native vocabulary class — adding an ingredient that breaks the plus/minus
> symmetry, and adding that ingredient *is* assuming the answer. The direction of time (the
> issuance/finality arrow) cannot substitute for it: the arrow and the sign are independent binary
> freedoms at every tier modeled. So the sign is
> **located** — as a fiber over the consistency-and-finality structure, with a named physics home
> (the sector-asymmetric spectral condition, i.e. energy positivity) — but **not forced**. Whether nature
> supplies the symmetry-breaking ingredient is an open physical question, exactly as open as when the
> program started, but now with its shape, price, and address fully specified.

## 2. The theorem, technical form

> **Bounded Fiber Theorem (toy-family grade, 2026-07-16).** Let the sign datum be the ℤ/2 fiber of link 1.
> Then at every tier modeled to date — value-blind profiles (3), field-valued transport (4), non-thin
> morphisms and partitioned transport (5), TaF's shipped T26 patch machinery (6), and every functional
> definable over a vocabulary on which the global flip acts as an automorphism (8, the stipulated
> formalization of "finality/issuance-native") — the fiber is **transported without insertion and
> undetermined**: no structure at any of these tiers selects a fiber point. Deriving the **orientation**
> requires a flip-symmetry-breaking primitive, whose addition is equivalent to positing the datum (8);
> the issuance/finality **arrow** cannot substitute, being an independent ℤ/2 (10); and under partitioned
> transport the burden is **per-block**, (ℤ/2)^blocks (5). The datum's physics-side name is the
> sector-asymmetric spectral condition (11). The datum itself — and whether nature supplies the
> flip-odd primitive — is `bar(b)`-grade **OPEN** (13, and GU-001's own nonclaims).

## 3. Enumerated escapes (the doors, each with its price)

1. **Non-binary value alphabets (|V| ≥ 3).** Link 6's classification lemma is binary-alphabet; for larger
   alphabets "label-free" is strictly stronger than any single-involution evenness. A constructive success
   there would be *outside* the lemma, not in conflict with it. Price: exhibit the alphabet's physical
   meaning and rerun the classification.
2. **Vocabulary choice / richer frameworks.** Link 8's no-go is class-relative and its class is stipulated;
   the L6 residue was *relocated into vocabulary choice*, not closed. A natural primitive failing the
   flip-automorphism battery is not thereby refuted — it is *characterized* as carrying one ℤ/2 bit. Price:
   argue the primitive is legitimate finality-native physics rather than the posit renamed — which, per
   Located-Is-Not-Forced, is nature's question, not the toys'.
   **2a. Finite presentation.** The no-go battery decides only candidates presented as finite tables over
   the g-closed domains; a construction native to new sorts or infinite domains (e.g. actual matrix-vector
   application, which the vocabulary omits) is not battery-decidable as-is — the presentation step is where
   an escape would hide. Price: supply the finite presentation and rerun the battery.
3. **Native-formalism tiers.** The toys stipulate encodings. TaF's native primitives beyond shipped T26
   (and TI's `OnlineIssuance^LC` at Lean grade) have not been battery-tested for flip-equivariance at
   formalism grade; the enriched-toy tier (5) also leaves T24 time/scale labels and site-map families only
   partially exercised, and two FORMALISM-declared T26 components (scalar/vector projections,
   `compare_systems`) are covered only at source-audit grade. Price: build the native-tier battery.
4. **The physical door (the real one).** Whether nature supplies a flip-odd primitive — energy positivity /
   the spectral condition — is the open physics. All roads priced it; none walked it. This door belongs to
   physics (GU's source action, or an experimental discriminator), not to this repository.
