# Possibility to Capability

Status: active public research repository.

This directory is the public research home for the neutral, repo-independent
layer connecting possibility, dynamics, records, access, capability, and the
open question of finality.

Joe ratified the name, charter, and `AGENTS.md` on 2026-07-14. The standard
repository steward package is active for Mailbox Processing, Repo Progress,
Repo Stewardship, and Repo Discovery. Routine versioning lives at
`https://github.com/disruptionjoe/possibility-to-capability`.

## Distinctive agent-capability role

The repository tests whether research agents can determine what kind of change
actually occurred. In particular, it asks them to distinguish a changed
description from a changed record, access condition, resource budget,
executable task set, containment claim, or finality claim. The result may be a
typed classification, a scoped disagreement, or an honest abstention.

Physics provides unusually sharp test cases, but it is not the scope of the
program. The same definitions must work on source-grounded cases in
computation, security, organizations, institutions, biology, and other domains
without changing meaning to obtain the intended answer.

## Operating boundary

- Do not edit GU Formalization, Temporal Issuance, Time as Finality, or WI-069
  truth from work mounted here.
- Import cross-repo evidence only as frozen, provenance-bearing packets.
- Treat each source repository's verdict as sovereign.
- Never combine partial results into an artificial joint success.
- Preserve null, negative, incompatible, and `NOT_YET_IMPORTABLE` outcomes.

## Current research position

Serialized Progress swings have established provisional, repo-owned machinery
for:

- reproducible Frozen-Packet v0.2 identity and explicit method dependencies;
- a neutral eight-gate evaluation contract;
- adversarial transition diagnosis across possibility, dynamics, records,
  access, normalized capability, and candidate finality;
- accepting frozen sovereign packets without moving their source verdicts;
- a bounded coherent-story hypothesis and ranked decisive tests; and
- finite-model physical specimens involving a superconducting ring and neutral
  BEC circulation, plus a model-relative completion-class firewall.

The strongest warranted synthesis treats the hierarchy as a fallible typed
diagnostic, not a chronology, causal ladder, ontology, or established physical
result. The physical specimens establish neither irreducible issuance nor a
general capability claim. Their principal value is that they make the
classification and its strongest completion rival executable. The next
generality test is a preregistered, source-grounded nonphysics transition using
the same definitions and an explicit abstention path.

Start with `synthesis/2026-07-14-coherent-story-v0.1.md` for the plain-English
picture, `experiments/2026-07-14-ranked-decisive-test-program-v0.1.md` for the
next tests, and `governance/CHARTER.md` plus `AGENTS.md` for boundaries.

Progress uses two research lanes. Lane 1, Hierarchy Force or Falsify, is the
protected North Star. Lane 2, Hierarchy Hardening, owns
definitions, literature comparison, casebook and counterexamples, benchmark
design, independent-reader reproducibility, and publication-oriented
byproducts. Stewardship is administrative rather than a third research lane.
The North Star is not demoted for difficulty or a class-relative wall; see
`AGENTS.md`.

## First-Class Lanes

Load root `LANES.yaml` after this repository's governance and before selecting
work. It is the owner-authoritative source for durable Lane definitions,
admission, and normal control state; authoritative work remains at the paths it
references. Numbered Lanes are Progress, lettered Lanes are Stewardship, and
Discovery is Lane-less. A direct mount uses these local surfaces without
CapacityOS. System observations, health, schedules, and execution history are
not Lane truth.
